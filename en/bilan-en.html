Strengths of the Year

  • Passionate Teaching: Our former teacher, highly skilled and passionate, successfully conveyed in-depth knowledge in both theory and practice.
  • Enriching Labs: The practical labs were very constructive and allowed us to go far in both theory and practice.
  • Balanced Theory/Practice: The courses offered a good balance between theory and hands-on practice, essential for our SISR training.

Areas for Improvement

  • Length of Labs: The labs were sometimes too long, which could make their completion complex within a limited timeframe.

Improvement Goals for 2025-2026

  • More Concise Labs: Shorter labs could facilitate the assimilation of concepts without compromising quality.
  • Deeper Practice: Continue to prioritize real-life situations, to better prepare for professional life.
